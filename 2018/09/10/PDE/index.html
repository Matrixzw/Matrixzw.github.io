Problem 5

设 $\Omega_{h}={x_i\mid 0\le i\le m}$ ，其中 $x_i=a+ih,h=(b-a)/m$ 。又设 $u={u_i\mid 0\le i\le m}$ 为 $\Omega_h$ 上的网格函数，且 $u_0=0$ ，证明:
$$\Vert u\Vert_{\infty}\le \sqrt{b-a}|u|_1,\quad \Vert u\Vert\le \frac{b-a}{\sqrt{2}}|u|_1$$

ans.

  1. $$\forall i ,u_i^2=\left(\sum_{l=0}^m\delta_x u_{i+1/2}\right)^2h^2\le m\sum_{l=0}^{m-1}(\delta_xu_{i+1/2})^2h^2\le(b-a)|u|_1^2$$

从而 $\Vert u\Vert_{\infty}^2=\max u_{i}^2\le(b-a)|u|_1^2$

即有 $\Vert u\Vert_{\infty}\le\sqrt{b-a}|u|_1$

  1. 由于
    $$|u_j|^2=\left(\sum_{k=1}^j\delta_xu_{k-1/2}\right)^2h^2\le j\sum_{k=1}^j(\delta u_{k-1/2})^2h^2$$
    同理有：
    $$|u_m|^2\le m\sum_{k=1}^m(\delta_xu_{k-1/2})^2h^2$$
    从而：
    $$\begin{align}\Vert u\Vert^2&=h\left(\sum_{j=1}^m|u_j|^2-\frac{1}{2}u_m^2\right) \\
    &=h \left(\sum_{j=1}^mj\sum_{k=1}^j(\delta_x u_{k-1/2})^2h^2-\frac{m}{2}\sum_{k=1}^m(\delta_xu_{k-1/2})^2h^2\right)\\
    &=h\left(\sum_{k=1}^m \sum_{j=k}^m j(\delta_x u_{k-1/2})^2h^2-\frac{m}{2}\sum_{k=1}^m(\delta_xu_{k-1/2})^2h^2\right)\\
    &=\frac{h^3}{2}\sum_{k=1}^m(m^2+k-k^2)(\delta_xu_{k-1/2})^2\\
    &\le \frac{n^2h^3}{2}\sum_{k=1}^m(\delta_xu_{k-1/2})^2=\frac{(b-a)^2}{2}|u|_1^2\end{align}$$

即：
$$\Vert u\Vert\le \frac{b-a}{\sqrt{2}}|u|_1^2$$

Problem 6

设 $u={u_i\mid 0\le i\le m}\in V_h$ 。证明 :

  • $$\Vert u\Vert_{\infty}^2\le 2\max {u_0^2,u_m^2}+\frac{b-a}{2}|u|_1^2$$
  • 对任意的 $\epsilon>0$ 有
    $$\Vert u\Vert_{\infty}^2\le \epsilon |u|_1^2+\left(\frac{1}{\epsilon}+\frac{1}{b-a}\right)\Vert u \Vert^2$$
  1. $$\forall,i v_i=h\sum_{j=1}^i\delta_xv_{j-1/2}+v_0=-h\sum_{j=i+1}^m\delta_xv_{j-1/2}+v_m$$

利用 $(a+b)^2\le2(a^2+b^2)$ ，得：
$$v_i^2\le 2h^2i\sum_{j=1}^i(\delta x)v_{j-1/2}^2+2v_0^2$$
$$v_i^2\le 2h^2(m-i)\sum_{j=i+1}^m(\delta_xv_{j-1/2})^2+2v_m^2$$

第一个式子乘以 $m-i$ 加上第二个式子乘以 $i$ ，得到：
$$\begin{align}mv_i^2 &\le 2h^2i(m-i)\sum_{j=1}^m(\delta_xv_{j-1/2})^2+2((m-i)v_0^2iv_m^2)\\
&\le \frac{m^2h^2}{2}\sum_{j=1}^m(\delta_xv_{j-1/2})^2+2((m-i)v_0^2+iv_m^2)\end{align}$$

即有 $$v_i^2\le \frac{b-a}{2}|v|_1^2+2\max \{v_0,v_m\}$$

取 $i$ 的最大值就有：
$$\Vert v\Vert_{\infty}^2\le \frac{b-a}{2}|v|_1^2+2\max\{v_0,v_m\}$$

  1. 设 $v_i$ 中当 $\max v_i=v_k,\min v_i =v_l$ 不妨就设 $k>l$
    则
    $$\begin{align}v_k^2&=\left(\sum_{i=l}^{k-1}\delta_xv_{i+1/2}^2\right)h+v_l^2\\
    &=2h\sum_{i=l}^{k-1}v_{i+1/2}\delta_xv_{i+1/2}+v_l^2\\
    &\le h\sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\frac{1}{\epsilon}v_{i+1/2}^2+\epsilon(\delta-x v_{i+1/2})^2+v_l^2\\
    &\le h\sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\frac{1}{\epsilon}v_{i+1/2}^2+\epsilon(\delta_xv_{i+1/2})^2+\frac{1}{b-a}\Vert v\Vert^2\\
    &=(\frac{1}{\epsilon}+\frac{1}{b-a})\Vert v\Vert^2+\epsilon|v|_1^2\end{align}$$
    其中利用 $$\Vert v\Vert^2=h(\frac{1}{2}v_0^2+\frac{1}{2}v_m^2+\sum_{i=1}^mv_i^2)\ge mhv_l^2=(b-a)v_l^2$$
